The question I get asked most is, "Where on earth did get the idea to make cookies with real salmon?" The answer is really quite simple. My childern bought me a cookie cutter shaped like a fish for Mothers Day. In one of my many conversations with my friends about our kids, the subject of odd items gifted by children came up. I brought up the salmon cookie cutter and one of my friends remarked, "You should make cookies with real salmon and make your kids eat them!" We all laughed but wouldn't you know it, I foumd myself whipping up my first batch of salmon cookies the very next day.

"Cookies so good, you'll be hooked"
